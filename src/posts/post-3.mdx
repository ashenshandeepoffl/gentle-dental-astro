Everyone knows that candy causes cavities, but there are several "healthy" foods that might be doing secret damage to your enamel.

### Citrus Fruits
Lemons, grapefruits, and oranges are packed with Vitamin C, but their high acid content can erode enamel over time. Always drink a glass of plain water after consuming citrus to wash away the acid.

### Dried Fruits
Raisins and dried apricots are sticky like caramel. They cling to the deep grooves of your molars, giving bacteria a long-lasting sugar source to feast on.
