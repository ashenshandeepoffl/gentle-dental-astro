The internet is full of DIY teeth whitening hacks, from charcoal toothpaste to lemon juice. But do they actually work, or are they damaging your smile?

### The danger of abrasive DIYs
Methods like baking soda and charcoal may scrub away surface stains, but they are highly abrasive. Over time, they literally scratch away your white enamel, exposing the yellow dentin underneath.

### Professional Whitening
The safest and most effective way to whiten teeth is through professional, peroxide-based treatments. At Gentle Dental, we offer custom-fitted trays that ensure the whitening gel stays on your teeth and away from your sensitive gums.
