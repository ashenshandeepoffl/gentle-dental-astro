We often put off dental visits until something hurts, but pain is usually the last symptom of a problem. Catching issues early can save you time, money, and discomfort.

### 1. Persistent bad breath
If brushing and mouthwash aren't fixing your bad breath, it could be a sign of early gum disease or a hidden cavity trapping bacteria.

### 2. Sensitivity to hot or cold
A sudden wince when drinking ice water or hot coffee usually means your enamel has worn down or you have an exposed root. Don't wait for it to become a constant ache.

### 3. Bleeding when you brush
Gums should never bleed during normal brushing. This is the hallmark sign of gingivitis, which is completely reversible if caught early by your dentist.
